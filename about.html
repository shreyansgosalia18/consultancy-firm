From Our Director's Desk

Presently, TechSkills core group has 4 members with varied skillsets. Our product portfolio presently has IAM [Identity and Access Management] and AI powered Advanced Analytics App for cloud platforms.Our Online Portal[techskillsit.com] also has around than 1500 enrollments.

Many companies in India needed external consulting advice on critical development paths. Around 2016, digital transformation was the keynote topic of most conferences, thats when I started providing expert consultancy service on complex technical grounds

Initially TechSkillsIT.com was created to support my classroom based courses but soon started having its own independent student base wanting to learning and enhance their skillsets.

By 2017, Java had a completely new world of technologies. Upgrading workforce skillsets soon became a complex challenge for most companies. Along with technical planning and architecture, we offered development services assisting existing programmers with new technologies.

Companies need to develop platforms along with products for cloud. SaaS model is soon becoming hard to ignore even for established companies. Real time predictive analytics is giving fortune companies big edge over SMEs. Our products are built to bridge that gap.
